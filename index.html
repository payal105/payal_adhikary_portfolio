Billing Management System

This project is based on billing management which is the most vital aspect of any business like grocery store , malls or restaurants. This Billing Management System application will help businesses to add new buyers, update their records in future, delete the buyers if they are not a part of their business anymore, add new products to keep track of the inventory, update the products with respect to price and availability, delete the products that can't be provided to the customers and finally comes with a wonderful billing system which generates bills as pdf and finds users from the database by their name's initials or contact number. Also, the products that are not available doesn't get displayed on the billing screen. It calculates the total amount to be paid along with the amount that the shop owner must return to the customer in case the customer had paid an extra amount. It also has the features to login, logout and close application. If installed in a shop, cafe or mall, this can prove beneficial and can provide a convenient way for the billing process.
**Note: The bills are generated in E Drive of your system. **
